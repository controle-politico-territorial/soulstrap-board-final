The name is derived from fresh and rest, with an implied sense of best —reflecting a balanced approach to everyday care that feels calm, clean, and intentional. The brand focuses on creating a moment of pause in daily routines — a quiet, refreshing ritual that reconnects the body and mind. The visual identity is based on simplicity and clarity.
